The understanding

What hosting here involves. Requesting a date means you're good with all of it.

1

The house fund

Each gathering contributes to cleaning, consumables, and upkeep, scaled to its size.

2

Every gathering has a producer

The requesting host, or someone from their community, handles setup, reset, and cleanup.

3

Two weeks' notice

Requests need at least two weeks so dates and overlaps can be sorted.

4

Limited cadence

A set number of gatherings each month, with occasional blackout dates.

5

Left as found

Clean, restocked, and put back. Anything used up is replaced.

6

Someone documents it

Each gathering is photographed, by your documentarian or an assigned one, for the archive.
